V-Connect

V-Connect is a smart home project for Vestel which produces electronic appliances and is one of the leading brands in Turkey. Vestel aims to make the latest tech available to all, in a new sub-branding. This project's goals are to offer solutions that make daily life easier, to convey to the target audience that Vestel can produce smart products at least as high quality as other brands in the world. Products which is contained in the context of the V-Connect project are smart socket, light, air conditioner, robot vacuum cleaner, washing machine.
